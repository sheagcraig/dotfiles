pleasure.
		-- Ambrose Bierce, "The Devil's Dictionary"

Molecule, n.:
	The ultimate, indivisible unit of matter.  It is distinguished
from the corpuscle, also the ultimate, indivisible unit of matter, by
a closer resemblance to the atom, also the ultimate, indivisible unit
of matter ... The ion differs from the molecule, the corpuscle and the
atom in that it is an ion ...
		-- Ambrose Bierce, "The Devil's Dictionary"
